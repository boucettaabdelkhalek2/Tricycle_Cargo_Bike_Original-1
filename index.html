Tricycle Cargo Bike

This open-source project documents the design and manufacturing process of a tricycle cargo bike suitable for urban and rural freight transportation.

Technical Specifications

  • Max Load Capacity: 150 kg
  • Number of Wheels: 3 (26 inches each)
  • Frame Material: Steel
  • Cargo Box: Front-mounted, made of wood
